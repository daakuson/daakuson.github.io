Meet
Jason Nusaputra

Jason Nusaputra is a 21 Year Old Illustrator and Graphic Designer from Indonesia, Jakarta.

He attended Universal School Kemayoran for High School, UTS Insearch for Foundation and Fresh Graduate in Raffles Design Institute with over 10 years of experience in Drawing, 6 years in Illustration and 4 years of Graphic Design.

 

He can recall drawing for the majority of my childhood and being inspired to make and design anything. Every year I have always been drawing and designing having noticable improvement each year. He Believes the Three Quotes that Describes him:

 

  • - No Matter What Happens Atleast You Have Experience it.
  • - Life is like a Game, So Play Smart.
  • - Work Hard and Smart not work Smarter not Harder.
