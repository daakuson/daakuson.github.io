PIZZA-LA


Pizza-La (ピザーラ, Pizāra) is the second highest-grossing pizza chain in Japan. The company has its headquarters in the Zenkaren Building (全菓連ビル, Zenkaren Biru) in Minami Aoyama, Minato, Tokyo.

The company's slogan is "All the taste and toppings you want on a pizza, straight from our oven to your door!" The company's mascot is Pizza-La-Kun. The name of the company combines "Pizza" and "Godzilla".

TASTE

It taste chewy and crisp, with marinara sauce and cheese. However you mostly taste the marinara and the crispy chewiness of the dough then the cheese. Its all about the textures combining with the flavour of the sauce to give you a umami effect.
